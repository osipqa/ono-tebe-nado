About the Auction

Here you won't find another pretentious gathering of incredibly expensive antiques. Our lots weren't supposed to be at any auction at all. Because who needs a teenage girl's diary or a painting that even a child can replicate? Who needs all these strange things created by amateurs?

Too complex or, on the contrary, too simple. Ahead of their time. Torn to shreds by critics. Unaccepted and misunderstood. Once they seemed merely unsuccessful. But despite the criticism, seeming simplicity or complexity, now these lots are indispensable to contemporary culture. When no one believed in these things, their creators continued to believe. And now these lots have become cult.
